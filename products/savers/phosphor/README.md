# Phosphor

Phosphor is the vector-and-phosphor product in the Framebuffer and Vector Family.

It provides:

- three related curve families: lissajous, harmonograph, and dense phosphor traces
- persistence as a first-class part of the product identity through a real software trail buffer
- modest drift and mirror controls with curated presets instead of a general plotting package
- themes ranging from amber and green phosphor to a drafting-board presentation
- preview-safe and long-run-stable behavior through the shared saver and renderer contracts
- `C13` adds stronger amber and white instrument variants, calmer long-run ratio refresh, improved preview-safe pacing, and a more deliberate laboratory presentation without widening the product into a formula editor

Phosphor stays intentionally bounded.
It is not a general graphing tool, math-expression parser, or broad vector engine.
