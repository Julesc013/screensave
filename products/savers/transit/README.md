# Transit

Transit is the route-and-infrastructure saver in the Places and Atmosphere Family.

It provides:

- curated motorway, rail-corridor, and harbor-night travel studies with clear forward progression
- modest speed and light-density controls instead of a driving simulator or map system
- wet-motorway, freight-run, neon-tunnel, harbor-lights, motorway-fog, harbor-midnight, and quiet-industrial themes and presets
- calmer preview-safe light reseeding and route sway so long runs stay cinematic rather than busy
- deterministic startup when requested and preview-safe long-run behavior through the shared saver and renderer contracts

Transit stays intentionally bounded.
It is not a vehicle simulator, a city generator, or a route editor.
