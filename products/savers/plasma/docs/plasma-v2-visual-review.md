# Plasma v2 Visual Review

Status: preview review protocol.

Plasma v2 review is based on deterministic preview captures and proof receipts.
It judges whether the current preview slice is useful enough to continue as a
product candidate, not whether it is stable or released.

Review dimensions:

- composition
- motion quality
- palette and material quality
- luminance safety
- dark-room comfort
- control influence
- distinctness
- artifacting
- degradation honesty
- preview and fullscreen suitability

Decision classes:

- `accepted-for-preview`
- `request-changes`
- `rejected`
- `defer-to-labs`

Explicit non-decisions:

- stable release
- compatibility certification
- Core promotion
- all-platform support
- public SDK stability

The review input is the Plasma v2 reference-preview contact sheet, proof bundle,
materials summary, and control-influence summary. A preview decision may advance
the slice to reviewed-preview, but release promotion remains blocked until a
later product series records separate release evidence.
