# Plasma v2 Stable-Promotion Policy

Status: stable-promotion policy, not publication.

Plasma v2 may become stable only after the stable-promotion gate checks the
release-candidate contract, proof bundle, final visual acceptance, native
artifact package, support wording, known limits, security/provenance review,
Manager inspection, Workbench inspection, and repair queue status.

Stable promotion admits the bounded Plasma v2 slice:

- product: Plasma v2
- profile: `plasma.v2.reference.preview`
- default material: `plasma_lava`
- renderer floor: `gdi`
- first optional acceleration candidate: `gl11`
- pack status: data-only `packc` v1-candidate

Stable promotion does not admit every historical Plasma experiment. Premium,
glyph, ribbon, heightfield, broad renderer lanes, public SDK stability,
executable plug-ins, and all-saver migration remain outside this product scope.

Publication remains separately gated.
