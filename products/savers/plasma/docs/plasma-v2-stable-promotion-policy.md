# Plasma v2 Stable-Promotion Policy

Status: stable-promotion policy, not publication.

Plasma v2 may become stable only after the stable-promotion gate checks the
release-candidate contract, instrument architecture, proof bundle, final visual
acceptance, native artifact package, support wording, known limits,
security/provenance review, Manager inspection, Workbench inspection, and
repair queue status.

The stable product center is direct controls, not preset selection. Presets are
examples, bookmarks, migration anchors, and curated starting points; they are
not hidden runtime authority after a direct Plasma v2 specification has been
resolved.

Stable promotion admits the bounded Plasma v2 slice:

- product: Plasma v2
- profile: `plasma.v2.reference.preview`
- default material: `plasma_lava`
- renderer floor: `gdi`
- first optional acceleration candidate: `gl11`
- pack status: data-only `packc` v1-candidate
- product architecture: direct controls to spec, plan, runtime, material,
  treatment, flat presentation, and proof

Stable promotion does not admit every historical Plasma experiment. Premium,
glyph, ribbon, heightfield, arbitrary graph runtime, hidden GL11 minimum,
broad renderer lanes, public SDK stability, executable plug-ins, and
all-saver migration remain outside this product scope.

Publication remains separately gated.
