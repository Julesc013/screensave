# Plasma U09 Release Notes Draft

## Scope Of The Current Release Posture

This Plasma cut ships as a unified runtime with a preserved Classic-first
default path, a truthful lower-band baseline, a narrower stable content and
control surface, and an explicit experimental slice for broader lanes and
grammar studies.

## What Is Preserved

- Plasma remains `Plasma`.
- `Plasma Classic` remains preserved inside the product as content identity and
  compatibility surface.
- Default preset remains `plasma_lava`.
- Default theme remains `plasma_lava`.
- The stable routing posture remains `minimum_kind=gdi`,
  `preferred_kind=gl11`, and `quality_class=safe`.
- The stable default visual path remains raster-plus-flat.
- The stable default promise remains lower-band-first rather than premium-first.

## What Is Now Stable

The stable cut is intentionally narrow.
It now includes:

- one unified runtime instead of a split Classic runtime story
- truthful Basic settings for the shipped stable subset
- the `classic_core` stable preset pool
- the `classic_core` stable theme pool
- the `classic_core_featured` and `dark_room_support` stable curated slices
- the `deterministic_classic` and stable `dark_room_support` support profiles
- explicit requested, resolved, and degraded reporting
- read-only support and audit tooling through BenchLab, Plasma Lab, and stable
  PX41 metadata reports

## What Is Available As Experimental

The following remain implemented but explicitly experimental:

- richer lanes beyond `gl11`
- Advanced and Author/Lab access to broader grammar
- banded and contour output families
- glyph output families
- direct treatment-family controls
- `heightfield` and `ribbon`
- transitions, journeys, and timing controls
- `wave3_experimental_sampler`
- experimental bridge, collection, and control-profile metadata
- capture-backed compare and capture-backed degrade inspection over text
  captures

## Lower-Band Compatibility Posture

The stable release posture remains centered on:

- `minimum_kind=gdi`
- `preferred_kind=gl11`
- `quality_class=safe`

Higher lanes remain optional uplifts.
They are not the hidden minimum and they are not silently part of the stable
promise.

## Major Caveats And Known Limits

- proof still comes from one capable validation machine
- no universal screenshot or perceptual diff framework exists
- transitions remain real but only partially proved
- glyph output remains intentionally narrow
- premium presentation remains bounded and caveated
- richer lanes remain experimental
- live `suite` and `anthology` behavior is not being claimed
- automation, curation, and provenance remain local and bounded
- numeric performance SLAs are still not claimed

## Release Tone Boundary

This draft is intentionally factual.

It does not claim broad stable widening, broad ecosystem maturity, or
cross-hardware certainty that the repo does not actually prove.
