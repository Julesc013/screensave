# Plasma U09 Ship Posture

## Purpose

`U09` records the final ship-posture decision for Plasma after the full
corrective program.

## Maintainer Executive Summary

Decision: **GO WITH CAVEATS**

Plasma is shippable now as:

- a narrow stable runtime product centered on the preserved Classic-first
  lower-band path
- a truthful stable support surface through BenchLab reporting, read-only
  Plasma Lab audits, and stable-only PX41 metadata reports
- a broader but explicitly opt-in experimental slice for richer lanes,
  non-raster grammar, premium presentation, and bounded transition behavior

This closes the corrective program cleanly as a curation and decision pass.
It does not erase the remaining caveats.

## Explicit Decision

Decision: **GO WITH CAVEATS**

## Concise Rationale

The decision remains `GO WITH CAVEATS` because:

- the stable default path is now narrow, explicit, and strongly evidence-backed
- the repo now has one runtime, truthful settings authority, and a smaller,
  retuned stable content surface
- the repo now has a formal proof substrate and a formal QA bar
- the repo now has explicit acceptance decisions rather than implied promotion
- but richer lanes, transitions, glyph output, and premium presentation still
  remain bounded or caveated rather than broadly stable
- and the proof envelope still depends on one-machine breadth plus a bounded
  render-signature and text-capture model rather than a broad screenshot-grade
  lab

## Strongest Remaining Caveats Or Blockers

- proof still comes from one capable validation machine rather than a broad
  cross-driver sweep
- no universal screenshot or perceptual diff framework exists
- transitions, journeys, and timing controls remain only partially proved
- glyph output remains bounded to a narrow admitted subset
- `heightfield` and `ribbon` remain premium-aware and lower-lane-caveated
- richer lanes beyond `gl11` remain explicitly experimental
- the authored preset and theme registry remains partly code-anchored
- stable preset and theme crowding is improved but not fully gone
- PX41 integration and curation remain metadata-first and report-first rather
  than live-consumer proved
- numeric performance SLAs still do not exist

## What This Decision Means

This decision means:

- Plasma can now be released honestly with a narrow stable promise
- the README and release-note story can speak clearly about what is stable,
  what remains experimental, and what is non-claim
- maintainers now have a current living recut that supersedes the earlier PX50
  posture docs

## What This Decision Does Not Mean

This decision does not mean:

- every implemented lane or visual-grammar surface is now stable
- transitions are now part of the stable default promise
- glyph or premium presentation are now broad stable features
- live `suite` or `anthology` behavior is now being claimed
- the repo now has no caveats left

## Scope Boundary

`U09` is a curation and decision phase only.

It does not change the runtime to force a better posture.
It names the posture the current repo has actually earned.
